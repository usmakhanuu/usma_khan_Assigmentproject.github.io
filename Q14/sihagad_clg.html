Sinhgad College of Engineering

Commonly called SCOE, Sinhgad College of Engineering Pune is situated in Vadgaon, Pune. Formed in 1996, this private institution of higher education is officially certified with Savitribai Phule Pune University. The institute offers 4-year undergraduate engineering programmes. The institute has 6 engineering departments and it also offers postgraduate programmes that lead to an MBA degree. It has a total student enrolment of more than 1,500 students. It offers 8 undergraduate programmes and various PhD research centres. All the programmes offered at the institute are All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) approved.
