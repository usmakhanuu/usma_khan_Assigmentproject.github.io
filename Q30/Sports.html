Sports 

The University Board of Sports & Physical Education came into existence in the year 1949. 
The objective of the Board of Sports & physical Education 
is to take necessary steps for promotion of sports culture and activities
 in the field of sports in colleges and university departments and also to establish links with regional


 and national bodies in the various sports and to promote various activities jointly with them in colleges,
 recognized institutions and university departments as per the policy of the university.
